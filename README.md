# AutomatedBlind

It's my first huge project done :)

Materials :

- 1 Actuator 22 rpm
- 1 rolling blind 
- 1 solar pannels
- 1 battery 
- 1 regulator
- 2 ultrasonic sensors pick up level of the blind
- 1 luminosity sensor pick up the luminosity of the area
- 2 touch sensors to up and down the blind
- 2 relays
- Arduino card + shield
